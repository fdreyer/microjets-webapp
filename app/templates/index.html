Information

The fixed order predictions were obtained with NLOJet++, while the resummation is achieved with MicroJets, based partly on HOPPET.

MicroJets is the code developed for the study of small-R jets.

It has been used in arXiv:1411.5182 and arXiv:1602.01110.


Parameters

Proton-proton collisions.

Center of mass energy = 7 TeV.

PDF = CT10 NLO.

0.5 μ0 < μR, μF < 2 μ0 with μ0=pt,max(R=1).

Binning as used by ATLAS in arXiv:1410.8857.

K is the NNLO/NLO ratio at Rm=1.
